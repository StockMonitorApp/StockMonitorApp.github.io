StockMonitor App

StockMonitor brings the stock or index you're most interested in to your macOS menu bar. To have an even better sight on your investments you can show the current value of your stake. If the current stock price is higher than your buying price it turns green, when it's lower it turns red. It also shows the trend based on the last price one day before.
